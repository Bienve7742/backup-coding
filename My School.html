My School

I study at a school called Tunas Muda School Meruya at Indonesia. Tunas Muda is a Catholic IB school centered in Jakarta, Indonesia, established in 1994. Where the aim is to nurture young people through the teachings of God, faith, and love, and to provide them the skills to prepare them for the future and contribute as members of society.

The addreses for Tunas Muda School Kedoya is Jl. Angsana Raya D8/2 Taman Kedoya Baru, Kedoya, Jakarta 11520, while our Meruya campus is at Jl. Meruya Utara Raya No.71 Kembangan Jakarta 11620

This school consists of 245 students split by 107 boys and 138 girls.

This school consists of the languange stream (Bahasa Indonesia and English), Humaninites stream(Business Studies, Economics, Global Politics, and Psychology), Science Stream(Environmental Science, Biology, Chemistry, Computer Studies, and Physics), Maths Stream (Maths standard and extended), as well as the arts stream(Visual and Music).

Tunas Muda School's curriculum is IB or International Baccalaureate.
